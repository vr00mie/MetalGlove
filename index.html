Mechano-Invaders

Battle a variety of mechanized invaders with different behaviors and abilities. Destroy spawners to stop the invasion. Beware of the Security Bots and Mega-MechanoSpyders!

Pickups

Your world is filled with goodies to help you along your way -- from shot speed boosts to temporary invisibility and pulse bombs. Grab 'em before your friends do!

Teleporters and Traps

Be careful where you step! Trap tiles may open doors that release waves of monsters, or they may open doors that are standing between you and your goal. Use teleporters to explore unreachable areas. Can you clear the whole map?
