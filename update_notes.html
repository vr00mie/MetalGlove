Update Notes

Update 1.0.4

  • Fixed a wrong sprite sometimes being shown briefly when a door is destroyed.
  • Fixed a bug that was causing AI decision making to be too slow.
  • Fixed a bug with the player melee speed limiter.
  • Fixed a bug that was causing enemies to not scale properly with damage state (makes full-strength enemies stronger).
  • Adjusted balance and scoring due to changes in difficulty from bug fixes.
  • Tweaked tutorial text.
  • Added a music loop during gameplay.
  • Added more description of content in the world packs on the purchase screen.
  • Added a series of menu idle / post-credits screens that display information about the base enemies.
  • Fixed a bug that caused the plasma shot charging sound to get stuck if the game is paused while a player is charging a shot.
  • Hide the bundle pack from the purchase screen when a player has already purchased one of the included worlds, as purchasing the remaining worlds separately is cheaper.
